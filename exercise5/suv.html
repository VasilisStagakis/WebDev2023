The Nissan Juke is a subcompact crossover SUV produced by the Japanese car manufacturer Nissan since 2010.
Debuted as a production vehicle at the 2010 Geneva Motor Show in March and positioned below the C-segment Qashqai, it was introduced to North America at the 2010 New York International Auto Show to be sold for the 2011 model year.
